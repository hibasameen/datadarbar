About

This is a community-built explorer using Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS) 2023 census tables. Data are parsed from PBS tables and joined to a district GeoJSON.

  • Source: PBS 2023 Census (district tables 12 & 13).
  • Notes: Some districts may be missing where PBS tables don’t report or naming differs. Please raise issues on GitHub.
